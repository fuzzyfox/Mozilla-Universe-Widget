Mozilla Universe Widget by fuzzyfox

This is a small widget, created with jQuery due to nessesity, that allows you to embed the Mozilla universe map on any webpage. Note that this is experimental and may not be used for anything official as of yet!

Dependencies

jQuery 1.4.2
